Making OOH Data Accessible

We're building the measurement infrastructure the out-of-home advertising industry needs. Real data, delivered in real time, with privacy at its core.

Our Mission

Data Jam exists to bring transparency and accountability to out-of-home advertising measurement.

For too long, OOH media owners have relied on traffic surveys, statistical models, and estimates to demonstrate the value of their inventory. Advertisers have had to trust projections rather than see real performance data.

We believe OOH deserves better. That's why we built JamBox—a simple, privacy-compliant device that counts actual audience presence at any out-of-home location. No cameras, no personal data, no complex infrastructure. Just real impressions, counted in real time.

Our goal is to make OOH audience measurement as accessible and reliable as digital analytics. When media owners can prove their value with real data, and advertisers can verify their campaign performance, the entire industry benefits.

Why OOH Measurement Matters

Out-of-home advertising is experiencing a renaissance, but measurement hasn't kept pace.

While digital advertising offers granular, real-time analytics, OOH has traditionally relied on traffic studies conducted years ago, mobile location data with uncertain accuracy, or statistical models that estimate rather than measure.

This measurement gap creates challenges across the industry:

  • Media owners struggle to prove the true value of their inventory
  • Agencies can't easily compare OOH performance to digital
  • Advertisers lack the proof of performance they get from other channels
  • DOOH operators need real-time data for programmatic selling

Data Jam is closing this gap. By making real-time, accurate OOH audience measurement accessible and affordable, we're helping the industry compete on equal footing with digital channels.
