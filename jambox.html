What is JamBox?

JamBox is a compact, powerful OOH audience measurement device that counts impressions in real time. Unlike camera-based systems or modeled estimates, JamBox detects actual audience presence at your out-of-home advertising locations.

Designed specifically for OOH media owners, advertising agencies, and brands, JamBox delivers the footfall data and proof of performance that the out-of-home industry needs. From billboards to transit shelters, from street furniture to DOOH screens—JamBox measures them all.

Key Capabilities

  • Real-time impression counting — Data by the second, hour, or day
  • 300-meter detection range — Covers your entire OOH site
  • GDPR compliant — No cameras, no personal data
  • Predictive analytics — ML-powered forecasting after 6 months
  • Custom alerts — Notifications for traffic patterns

How JamBox Measures OOH Audiences

JamBox uses advanced sensor technology to detect mobile devices within range of your OOH asset. This provides accurate audience counts without cameras, surveys, or statistical modeling.

01

Device Detection

JamBox detects anonymized mobile device signals within a 300-meter radius. No personal data is collected—just aggregate counts of devices present.

02

Real-Time Processing

Data is processed and transmitted in real time. You can view audience counts by the second, minute, hour, or day through the Data Jam Portal.

03

Insights & Analytics

Access historical trends, peak hour analysis, and after 6 months, predictive forecasts based on weather, events, and behavioral patterns.

04

Proof of Performance

Generate reports showing verified audience counts, opportunity to see (OTS) metrics, and campaign performance data for advertisers.
